Operational Rules

Mandatory enforcement laws based on paired inlet/outlet readings

Law 01: Solid dilution mandate

If TSS or related solids rise above the acceptable limit, treated water must be pumped back into the system until compliance is restored.

Law 02 / 03: Residence time stop

If BOD or COD exceed the limit, inflow must be stopped and the plant must remain on hold until microbial degradation restores the readings.

Law 04 / 05 / 06 / 07: Chemical and hygiene control

pH, nitrate, TDS, and fecal coliform deviations trigger mandatory correction, discharge hold, or disinfection escalation before the system can resume.
